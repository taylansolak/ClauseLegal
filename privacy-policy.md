# Privacy Policy for Clause - Legal AI Assistant

**Effective Date:** April 23, 2026

At Clause, we value your privacy above all else. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use it.

## 1. On-Device Data Processing
Clause is designed to be a private-first application. 
- **No Document Uploads:** All legal documents, clauses, and texts you analyze using Clause are processed entirely on your device using Apple's CoreML technology.
- **On-Device AI:** Our AI models run locally on your iPhone/iPad. Your sensitive legal data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers or any third-party AI providers for processing.

## 2. Information Collection
- **Personal Data:** Clause does not require an account, email address, or any personally identifiable information (PII) to function.
- **Analytics:** We do not track your individual usage patterns or the content of the documents you analyze.

## 3. Data Security
Since your data remains on your device, it is protected by Apple's built-in iOS security features and encryption. We recommend keeping your device updated and using a secure passcode/FaceID.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
